Why do we choose a monthly Pricing Model

We choose a monthly pricing model because SEO is an ongoing process that requires consistent effort. Search engines frequently update their algorithms, and your competitors are always working to improve their rankings. By working on your SEO monthly, we can continuously optimize your website, adapt to changes, and steadily improve your results. This approach ensures long-term success and better returns on your investment.


How long should your plan be active

Your SEO plan can remain active for as long as you choose, but keep in mind that SEO typically requires time and is best approached as a continuous process for the following reasons:

  • Indexing Time
    Search engines need time to crawl and index changes.
  • Building Authority
    Gaining quality backlinks and content takes time.
  • Algorithm Updates
    Adjustments may be needed due to frequent updates.
  • Competitor Monitoring
    Analyzing competitors requires time for strategy adaptation.
  • Data Analysis
    Continuous improvement based on performance data is essential.
